below you will find the best pani-puri / golgappe recipe

  • refreshing street style snack
  • healthy filling option
  • explosion of flavors =)

Ingredients:

  1. Whole wheat / suji baked puris – 15 pieces

Filling:

  1. Boiled chickpeas or sprouts – 1 cup
  2. Boiled potatoes – ½ cup
  3. Finely chopped onion – ¼ cup
  4. Finely chopped cucumber – ¼ cup
  5. Chaat masala – ½ tsp
  6. Salt – as needed

Pani:

  1. Mint leaves – 1 cup
  2. Coriander leaves – ½ cup
  3. Green chili – 1
  4. Lemon juice – 1 tbsp
  5. Roasted cumin powder – 1 tsp
  6. Black salt – ½ tsp
  7. Stevia / little jaggery powder – optional
  8. Cold water – 2 cups

Steps:

  1. Grind mint, coriander, chili, and water.
  2. Add lemon juice, cumin powder, black salt, and sweetener.
  3. Refrigerate pani for 30 minutes.
  4. Mix chickpeas, potatoes, onion, cucumber, and spices.
  5. Crack puri top slightly.
  6. Add filling.
  7. Add pani and serve immediately.
