below you will find the best momo recipe

  • easy to make
  • fun to do
  • after its done, fun to eat =)

Ingredients:

  1. Whole wheat flour / multigrain flour – 1 cup
  2. Warm water – as needed
  3. Salt – pinch

Filling:

  1. Finely chopped cabbage – 1 cup
  2. Finely chopped carrot – ½ cup
  3. Finely chopped capsicum – ½ cup
  4. Finely chopped onion – ¼ cup
  5. Minced chicken / tofu / paneer (optional protein) – ½ cup
  6. Garlic – 1 tsp (finely chopped)
  7. Ginger – 1 tsp (finely chopped)
  8. Soy sauce (low sodium) – 1 tsp
  9. Black pepper – ½ tsp
  10. Olive oil / sesame oil – 1 tsp
  11. Salt – as needed

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, salt, and warm water.
  2. Knead into soft dough and rest for 20 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in pan.
  4. Add garlic and ginger → sauté lightly.
  5. Add vegetables and protein option.
  6. Cook on high flame for 3–4 minutes.
  7. Add soy sauce, pepper, and salt.
  8. Cool filling completely.
  9. Roll dough into thin circles.
  10. Add filling and fold into momo shape.
  11. Steam momos for 10–12 minutes.
