below you will find the best spring roll recipe

  • crispy and healthy
  • fun to prepare
  • super tasty snack =)

Ingredients:

  1. Whole wheat spring roll sheets / rice paper – 8 sheets
  2. Olive oil – 1 tbsp

Filling:

  1. Shredded cabbage – 1 cup
  2. Carrot – ½ cup
  3. Beans – ½ cup
  4. Capsicum – ½ cup
  5. Boiled shredded chicken / tofu – ½ cup
  6. Garlic – 1 tsp
  7. Low sodium soy sauce – 1 tsp
  8. Black pepper – ½ tsp
  9. Salt – as needed

Steps:

  1. Heat small amount of oil in pan.
  2. Add garlic and sauté lightly.
  3. Add vegetables and protein option.
  4. Stir fry for 3–4 minutes.
  5. Add soy sauce, pepper, and salt.
  6. Cool the filling.
  7. Place filling inside sheet.
  8. Roll tightly and seal edges with water.
  9. Bake at 180°C for 20 minutes OR air fry at 180°C for 12–15 minutes.
